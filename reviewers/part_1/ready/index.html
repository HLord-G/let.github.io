The General Education (GenEd) Part 1 Take a Test

The Part 1: General Education exam evaluates your overall academic proficiency across a comprehensive range of foundational subjects essential for effective teaching. It encompasses key areas such as English grammar, vocabulary, and figurative language; mathematics (including arithmetic, algebra, geometry, probability, and statistics); and science concepts in biology, earth science, physics, and chemistry. The exam also assesses your competence in Filipino language and literature and your understanding of social sciences, including Philippine history, government, sociology, human rights, and economics. Furthermore, it covers ICT fundamentals and general knowledge to ensure a holistic mastery of core educational disciplines necessary for teaching excellence.
Take time to prepare and review thoroughly. Do not rely on AI, as during the actual exam, only your own knowledge will matter. Once you click "Start," the timer will begin.
