Civil Service Exam Reviewer 2025 (Professional and Sub-Professional) Part 1

NUMBER SERIES

The Career Service Professional Examination is a comprehensive test designed to evaluate an individual’s readiness for public service roles in the Philippines. It measures a broad spectrum of competencies including mathematical reasoning—with topics like Number Series, Ratio and Proportion, and Problem Solving; verbal ability—such as Vocabulary, Sentence Completion, and Reading Comprehension; and logical analysis—covering Logical Conclusions, Assumptions, and Relationships. The exam also includes general knowledge questions that focus on the Philippine Constitution, RA 6713, Peace and Human Rights Concepts, and Environmental Issues. These sections aim to assess not only the applicant’s cognitive and analytical skills but also their awareness of key national values and governance principles, making it a crucial step for entering civil service positions.
