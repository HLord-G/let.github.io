LET Reviewer for General Education (Gen Ed)

General Education Part 1 Take a Test

The "Part 1 General Education" exam measures your overall academic competence by assessing a wide scope of essential subjects expected from a well-rounded educator. It covers key areas such as English grammar, vocabulary, and figurative language, mathematics (including arithmetic, algebra, geometry, probability, and statistics), and science concepts in biology, earth science, physics, and chemistry. It also evaluates your proficiency in Filipino language and literature, understanding of social sciences like Philippine history, government, sociology, human rights, and economics. Additionally, the exam includes ICT fundamentals and general knowledge to ensure you have a holistic grasp of core educational disciplines crucial for teaching.

General Education Part 2 Take a Test

The LET General Education reviewer covers English, Math, Science, Filipino, Social Studies, and General Knowledge. It includes topics like grammar, math problems, ecology, history, literature, and tech info essential for teaching.

General Education Part 3 Take a Test

The General Education (GenEd) reviewer is a comprehensive exam assessing your academic foundation across core subjects. It includes Filipino and English focusing on grammar, vocabulary, and figures of speech. Science covers Physics, Biology, and Chemistry concepts like matter, energy, cells, and chemical reactions. Social Sciences tackle history, values, and geography. Math checks problem-solving with ratios and areas, while Computer Fundamentals introduce basic tech skills. This reviewer prepares you not just to pass—but to excel with confidence.

General Education Part 4 Take a Test

The "Part 4 General Education LET Reviewers" exam measures your comprehensive understanding of foundational knowledge across a wide range of disciplines essential for future educators. It covers in-depth topics in Filipino Language and Literature—such as grammar, figures of speech, key authors, and classic works—alongside English language proficiency involving grammar rules, vocabulary, and rhetorical devices. The exam also includes Science subjects like physics, chemistry, biology, and earth science, testing concepts from inertia and energy conservation to moon phases and DNA-related conditions. In Mathematics, it evaluates skills in number theory, algebra, ratios, geometry, and permutations. The Social Sciences section checks your awareness of Philippine history, governance, economics, sociology, and psychology, while also integrating basic computer literacy to ensure a well-rounded academic readiness for the teaching profession. General Education Part 5 Take a Test

The "Part 5 General Education" exam measures a candidate’s overall academic competency by evaluating their understanding across multiple key disciplines. It covers social awareness and ethical values, testing knowledge on human rights, societal problems, and moral principles. It also assesses scientific literacy through questions on biology, physics, chemistry, and earth science, ensuring familiarity with health, energy, natural phenomena, and environmental issues. A strong focus is placed on mathematical reasoning, including algebra, geometry, percentages, and statistics. Moreover, it evaluates language proficiency in both Filipino and English, emphasizing grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, and literature. Lastly, it checks a candidate’s grasp of basic technology and educational concepts, such as computer use and internet safety—making the exam a broad and integrated test of general education.
